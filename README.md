# 📊 Target Brazil E-commerce Analysis (SQL Case Study)

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes Target Brazil’s e-commerce operations using SQL and Power BI to uncover insights related to growth, customer behavior, delivery performance, and revenue trends.

---

## 📈 Key Highlights

- 📊 227% growth in orders (2017–2018)
- 📈 137% increase in revenue (Jan–Aug YoY)
- 🚚 91.9% on-time delivery rate
- 💳 Credit cards dominate (~75% of transactions)
- 🌎 São Paulo contributes ~42% of total orders

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Growth is driven primarily by **increase in order volume**, not higher spending per order
- Delivery delays significantly impact customer satisfaction
- Strong seasonal demand observed during mid-year and festive periods
- High dependency on a single region (São Paulo) indicates expansion opportunity

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve logistics performance in high-delay states
- Expand into underpenetrated regions
- Increase Average Order Value through bundling and upselling
- Optimize marketing campaigns based on seasonality
- Diversify payment methods to reduce dependency on credit cards
